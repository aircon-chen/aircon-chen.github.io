The hardest part for a beginner? Instantly recognizing notes on a staff. My teacher would hand-draw random notes in a manuscript book for me to practice between lessons.

That's when it hit me — why not turn this into an app? It saves the teacher time drawing notes, and the student money buying practice books.

That's how SightReading was born — a simple tool built from a real need, designed to make sight-reading practice accessible anytime, anywhere.
